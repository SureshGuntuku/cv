NIRMALA SITHARAMAN

Early Life:

Nirmala Sitharaman was born in a - Tamil Iyengar family in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, to Savitri and Narayanan Sitharaman. She had her schooling from Madras and Tiruchirappalli. She obtained a Bachelor of Arts degree in economics at the Seethalakshmi Ramaswami College, Tiruchirapalli in 1980, Master of Arts degree in economics and M.Phil. from Jawaharlal Nehru University, Delhi in 1984.

Political Career:-

Sitharaman joined the BJP in 2006 and was appointed in 2010 as a spokesperson for the party. In 2014,she was inducted into Narendra Modi's cabinet as a junior minister and was elected in June of that year as a Rajya Sabha Member. Andhra Pradesh
